Behind The Lens

My journey behind the lens started back in the 11th grade with nothing but a smartphone and a curiosity for capturing landscapes and small details. That initial spark turned into a real obsession by my first year of college when I finally got my first DSLR. I spent those years practicing everything—from the quiet patience of wildlife and street photography to the high energy of portraits and live music.

After finishing college, I decided to stop treating photography as just a hobby and started taking it seriously as a career. Since then, I’ve been out there every day, working hard to find that one great opportunity to show what I can really do. Whether I’m freezing a split-second goal on the field or capturing the raw intensity of an event, I love the pressure of knowing you only have one shot to get it right.

Right now, I specialize in Sports, Events, and Wildlife photography. I don’t just take pictures; I look for those "perfect moments" that tell a story. From mastering fast shutter speeds on the field to the final touches in Lightroom and CapCut, I’m dedicated to making people feel the speed and emotion of the action. I’m looking for the right team, club, or media house where I can bring that hustle and help create something great.
